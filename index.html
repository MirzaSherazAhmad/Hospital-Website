The MUSE Cardiology Information System is more than a database management system: through seamless connectivity with resting ECG, exercise ECG, or Holter acquisition devices. MUSE integrates, manages, and streamlines the flow of cardiac information, enabling faster data delivery , distribution and analysis. In conjunction with the MAC VU360™, the latest version of MUSE provides enhanced workflow in the Emergency Department and pacemaker annotation capability.

MUSE supports your facility's bottom line, too. Improved billing processes help capture revenue. Bi-directional communication between your Hospital Information System (HIS), Electronic Medical Records (EMR) and ECG devices fosters enhanced workflow efficiencies throughout your healthcare facility, for better patient flow and faster reporting.
